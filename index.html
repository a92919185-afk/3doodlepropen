Frequently Asked Questions

Is 3Doodler safe for children? expand_more

Absolutely. Safety is our top priority. The 3Doodler Start+ (ages 6+) is specially designed with no hot parts ‚Äî both the pen tip and the plastic are safe to touch. For older creators (ages 14+), the Create+ has a hot tip but the plastic cools almost instantly.

Is this better than a regular 3D printer? expand_more

It's different! A traditional 3D printer requires software, 3D modeling skills, and waiting hours for a print. With 3Doodler, you are the printer. It offers instant gratification, fosters creativity, and enhances hand-eye coordination. It's much more accessible and fun for kids.

Do I need a computer or app to use it? expand_more

No! 3Doodler is a completely standalone creative tool. No apps, no wifi, and no computers required. Just charge it up (or plug it in), insert a strand of plastic, and start creating immediately.
