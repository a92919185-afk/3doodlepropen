Frequently Asked Questions

Is 3Doodler safe for children? expand_more

Absolutely. Safety is our top priority. The 3Doodler Start+ (ages 6+) is designed with no hot parts — both the pen tip and plastic are safe to touch. For older creators, the Create+ cools plastic almost instantly.

Does it require a computer or software? expand_more

No! 3Doodler is a standalone creative tool. You don't need any software, apps, or computers. Just plug it in, insert plastic, and start drawing in 3D immediately.

How long does shipping take to USA and Canada? expand_more

We offer fast, localized shipping. Orders within the contiguous USA typically arrive in 2-4 business days. Canadian orders ship from our Toronto warehouse and arrive in 3-5 business days with NO customs fees.

Is the plastic eco-friendly? expand_more

Yes, our Start+ plastic is non-toxic, BPA-free, and fully biodegradable. The PLA plastic for Create+ is made from cornstarch and is also biodegradable.
