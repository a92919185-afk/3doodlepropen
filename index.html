Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the world's best 3D pen.

Is 3Doodler safe for children? expand_more

Absolutely. Safety is our top priority. The 3Doodler Start+ (ages 6+) is specially designed with no hot parts — both the pen tip and plastic are completely safe to touch. For older creators, the Create+ cools plastic almost instantly.

Does it require a computer or app? expand_more

No! 3Doodler is a completely standalone creative tool. No apps, no wifi, and no computers required. Just plug it in, insert a plastic strand, and start drawing in 3D immediately.

What is the return policy and warranty? expand_more

We offer a 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund. We also provide a full 1-year limited warranty on the device.

How fast is shipping to US and Canada? expand_more

Orders within the USA typically arrive in 2-4 business days via FedEx/USPS. Canadian orders ship directly from our Toronto warehouse, arriving in 3-5 days with NO customs fees.
